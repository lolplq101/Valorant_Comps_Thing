April 6, 2026

Audit Fixes: Performance & UX Overhaul

Major infrastructure and usability updates based on a comprehensive platform audit.

  • Performance: Deferred non-critical JavaScript and added lazy loading for high-resolution map assets to significantly reduce Total Blocking Time (TBT).
  • Accessibility: Added extensive ARIA labels across Strat Board and Map Ban cards for assistive technology support.
  • V25 Clarity: Added clear UI badges to "Theoretical V25 Meta" agents (Veto, Miks, Tejo, Waylay) so players know they are speculative concepts and not yet in the live game.
  • Fixes: Resolved broken routing to the Strat Tools and Meta Report. Added this official Changelog page!

AI Map Ban Opponent

Added a new AI feature to the Draft block.

  • Set the AI's logic to target your specific "Strong" or "Weak" maps to practice difficult Veto scenarios.
